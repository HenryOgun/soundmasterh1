OrderFlow
Version 1.0 · By Henry Ogun

OrderFlow is a professional WhatsApp Order Manager built for small business owners, market traders, food vendors, and online sellers across Nigeria and Africa.

It helps you track customer orders received via WhatsApp, manage your inventory, generate receipts, and send professional order summaries back to customers — all from your phone.

📋
Order Management
Create, edit, track and update orders in real time
📲
WhatsApp Integration
Generate ready-to-send order summaries directly to customers
📦
Inventory Tracking
Monitor stock levels with low stock alerts
👥
Customer Directory
Build your customer list automatically as you take orders
🖨️
Print Receipts
Print or screenshot professional receipts for every order
📊
Sales Reports
View daily, weekly and monthly revenue summaries
